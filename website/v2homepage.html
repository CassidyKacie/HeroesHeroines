Come and See us at New York Comic Con.

New York Comic Con is an annual fan convention dedicated to comics, graphic novels, anime, manga, video games, toys, movies, and television. The convention is typically held in early October at the Javits Center in New York City, USA. The exact dates for New York Comic Con vary from year to year, but it usually takes place over four days, from Thursday through Sunday. The convention features a wide variety of events, including panel discussions with industry professionals, autograph signings, cosplay contests, and exclusive merchandise sales.

Come and meet the cast of Doom Patrol.

The Doom Patrol is a television series based on the DC Comics superhero team of the same name. The show premiered on DC Universe in February 2019 and later moved to HBO Max for its second and third seasons. The series follows a group of misfit heroes who have all suffered traumatic accidents that have left them with unusual abilities and disfigurements. Led by Dr. Niles Caulder, also known as the Chief, the team includes characters such as Robotman, Negative Man, Elasti-Girl, Crazy Jane, and Cyborg. The show explores themes of trauma, mental illness, and identity while blending humor and action. The Doom Patrol has received critical acclaim for its innovative storytelling and performances, particularly for the actors playing the characters with unusual physical appearances.

Come join us on May 6th, 2023 to get 5 free comics on Free Comic Book Day!

Free Comic Book Day is an annual event that takes place on the first Saturday in May. It was first held in 2002 as a way to promote comic book reading and bring new readers into the hobby. Participating comic book shops offer free comic books to customers, usually from a selection of specially-printed titles created for the event. These comics often include popular characters from Marvel, DC, and other publishers, as well as new and independent titles. Free Comic Book Day has become a popular event in the comic book community, attracting fans of all ages and providing a fun way for people to discover new comics and connect with fellow enthusiasts.
